# AI Workforce Risk Prediction

## Overview
This project analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on the job market by identifying high-risk jobs and emerging roles using machine learning techniques.

The study combines **predictive analytics** and **descriptive insights** to evaluate automation risk across industries and job roles.

---

## Objectives
- Identify industries most vulnerable to AI automation
- Predict job-level automation risk (High vs Not High)
- Discover emerging job roles and required skills
- Provide insights for workforce planning and reskilling

---

## Dataset
- Source: AI Job Market Insights Dataset (Kaggle)
- ~500 job records
- Features include:
  - Job Title
  - Industry
  - Company Size
  - Location
  - AI Adoption Level
  - Required Skills
  - Salary
  - Job Growth Projection
  - Automation Risk

---

## Key Results
- Stacking Ensemble achieved highest accuracy (~66%)
- High-risk industries:
  - Manufacturing
  - Finance
  - IT
- Low-risk industries:
  - Healthcare
  - Education

---

## Key Insights
- AI adoption strongly correlates with automation risk
- Routine and repetitive jobs are most vulnerable
- Emerging roles include:
  - Data Scientist
  - Machine Learning Engineer
  - AI Specialist
- High-demand skills:
  - Python
  - Data Analysis
  - Machine Learning
  - NLP
